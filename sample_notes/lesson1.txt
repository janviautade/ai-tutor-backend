Climate Change Notes

1. Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns.
2. The greenhouse effect is caused by gases like CO2, CH4, and N2O.
3. Renewable energy sources include solar, wind, and hydropower.
4. Deforestation increases carbon emissions and reduces biodiversity.
5. International agreements like the Paris Agreement aim to reduce global warming.


Seasons Notes
1. Summer is the hottest season of the year, usually between June and August in the Northern Hemisphere.
2. Winter is the coldest season, typically from December to February.
3. Spring is the season of new growth and flowers.
4. Autumn, or fall, is when leaves change color and temperatures drop.
